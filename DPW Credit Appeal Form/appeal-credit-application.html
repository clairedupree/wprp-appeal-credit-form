Credit Type: Property not under an NPDES permit
By Law, the maximum credit for any property is 50% of the Stormwater Remediation Fee. To receive the full 50% credit, the entire impervious surface area of the property must be treated using private on-site stormwater management practices that control at least one inch of rainfall and are designed, approved, constructed, and maintained in accordance with the County Standards, Stormwater Management Practices and Procedures Manual, and/or the 2000 Maryland Stormwater Design Manual, Volumes I and II and Supplements. If only a portion of the site's impervious area is treated or if the practices control less than one inch of rainfall, partial credit can be awarded.

Approved credits are valid up to a period of three years. To continue to receive credit, function verification of the BMP is required. This verification will automatically be conducted by the County and the owner will be notified only if the practice(s) is found to be out of compliance. A renewal application is not necessary.

Credit Type: Property with an NPDES permit that includes stormwater management controls
Properties that are regulated through a National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) permit with stormwater management controls are eligible for a stormwater remediation fee credit not to exceeed 50% of the assessed stormwater remediation fee. Marina properties are eligible for up to a 25% credit if they are regulated under an NPDES permit with stormwater management controls and up to a 50% credit if they are also in good standing with the Maryland Department of Natural Resources for the Clean Marina Initiative.
